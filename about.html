Our Story

FUSANQING CO., LTD was established with a vision to become a bridge connecting markets across the globe through reliable trade solutions. Based in Seoul, South Korea, we started as a small trading company and have since grown into a respected name in international commerce.

With our headquarters strategically located in Dongdaemun-gu, one of Seoul's major commercial districts, we have positioned ourselves at the heart of South Korea's dynamic economy while maintaining strong international connections.

Over the years, we have expanded our product portfolio and strengthened our global network, cementing our reputation as a versatile and dependable trading partner for businesses worldwide.
